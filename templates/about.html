About Us

Care Hub UK is a very reliable nursing agency for placing professionals in nursing and care jobs in and around the London, Surrey and Staffordshire regions, having a committed team of health staff who will expertly match your skills and work preferences to a variety of challenging and fulfilling positions. Leveraging a quality endorsement and strong relationships with both clients and staff, our main objective is to provide you with a paramount service of the highest standard, either on a long term or short term basis.
