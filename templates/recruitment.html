Recruitment

Care Hub Uk is an experienced Recruitment Consultants work closely with each candidate, with a personal, ethical and diligent approach. We work hard to understand your career aspirations and requirements and only progress applications that are deemed suitable.

Individual Recruitment :
Healthcare Assistants:
  • Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal.
  • Must be able to work on own initiative and as part of a team.
  • Must have the ability to complete detailed reports and documentation.
  • Capable of dealing with emergency situations in a calm manner.
  • Care experience is desirable, although training will be given to the right people.
  • A passion for care and making a difference to others.
RGNs/RMNs:
  • Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal.
  • Must be able to work on own initiative and as part of a team.
  • Must have the ability to complete detailed reports and documentation.
  • Capable of dealing with emergency situations in a calm manner.
  • Must have current NMC PIN
  • Must have previous experience
DBS:
  • All staff require a valid DBS (expense to be met by applicant, refundable after shifts have been completed)
Rates Of Pay:
  • Care Offers the most competitive rates in the industry, full details to be discussed during interview.
Self-Employed :
  • No problem - you are welcome to join our team and work with us.
